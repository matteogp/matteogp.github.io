Hack the North

Executive Assitant

I was fortunate enough to be able to join the Hack the North team input March 2017. Working together with a group of 36 other amazing people to help bring Canada's biggest hackathon to life has been an absolutely incredible experience for me both personally and professionally.

As the Executive Assitant, my role gave me the oppurtunity to quickly begin to acquire context on how a group of students can accomplish a task as daunting has accomodating over 1000 hackers and hundreds more volunteers, sponsors and judges from around the world to come together in one place for an unforgettable 36 hours.

My primary responsibilites include helping facilitate internal team communication, and ensuring all our documentation is in order to allow future teams to be able to look back at what we did this - helping them know what decisions we made, our reasons for doing so, and the results of them. In addtion to my day to day duties, I also had the flexibility to step up and take on more tasks involving the logsitics of the event as well as the work surrounding the judging process for submitted projects at the end of the event.

I've yet to be part of a team as effective, dedicated, motivated, and hardworking as Hack the North.
