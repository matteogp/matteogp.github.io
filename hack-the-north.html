Hack the North

Executive Assistant

I was fortunate enough to be able to join the Hack the North team in March, 2017. Working together with a group of 36 other amazing people to help bring Canada's biggest hackathon to life has been an absolutely incredible experience for me both personally, and professionally.

As the Executive Assistant, my role gave me the oppurtunity to quickly understand on how a group of students can accomplish a task as daunting has accomodating over 1000 hackers, hundreds more volunteers and sponsors, and over a dozen judges from around the world to come together in one place for an unforgettable 36 hours.

My primary responsibilites include helping facilitate internal team communication and ensuring all our documentation is accurate and accessbile to allow future years' teams to be able to look back at what we did this year - helping them know what decisions we made, our reasons for doing so, and the results of them. In addtion to my day to day duties, I also had the flexibility to step up and take on more tasks involving the logsitics of the event, as well as organizing many aspects of the judging process for submitted projects at the end of the event.
