Green & Gold Gala Ticketing

Online System for Ticket Purchase & Table Selection

The St. Jerome's University Students' Union hosts the annual Green & Gold Gala. It is a huge gathering of students, staff, and faculty; bringing the community together for one night before the end of the year.

Last year, our almost 300 guests all filled out a Google Form, then used a seperate spreadsheet to select their table. Shortly into the process, we recieved many requests about what tables will be where, or if we can place a table next to another, or who is near who as seating at the table was not being reflected by the spreadsheet. There was no way for people who are purchasing tickets to visualize the tables, leading to the many questions and requests that required accomodation.

As Chief Financial Officer, I am the lead for almost all things related to the Gala, including ticket sales. I believe that creating a new system dedicated to ticket sales would not only provide value to the team, but raise our profile in the St. Jerome's community by creating a more professional experience for our guests. Of course, this project also provides me with an oppurtunity ot learn more about both frontend and backend development - two fields which I am excited to gain more experience with.
