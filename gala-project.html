Green & Gold Gala Ticketing

Online System for Ticket Purchase & Table Selection

The St. Jerome's University Students' Union hosts the annual Green & Gold Gala. It is a huge gathering of students, staff, and faculty; bringing the community together for one night before the end of the year.

Last year, the almost 300 guests all filled out a google form, then used a seperate spreadsheet to select their table. Shortly into the process, we recieved many requests about what tables will be where, or if we can place a table next to another, or who is near who. Since we were using a spreadsheet, there was no way for people who are purchasing tickets to visualize the tables.

As Chief Financial Officer, I am the lead for almost all things related to the Gala, including ticket sales. I believe that creating a new system dedicated to ticket sales would not only provide value to the team, raise our profile in the St. Jerome's community, as well as provide me with an oppurtunity ot learn more about both frontend and backend development.
