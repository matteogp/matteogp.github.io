The Students’ Union is an independent, self-governing student society associated with St. Jerome’s University. The Students' Union operates collaboratively, on behalf of its members, with the Administration, and the Faculty and Staff of St. Jerome's University to enhance the overall student experience.
From the Students' Union Governing Constitution

St. Jerome's University (SJU) is a small, close-knit liberal arts school nestled next to the centre (and is part) of the University of Waterloo.

I was first elected to the Students' Union in Fall 2016 as the Buisnesss Operations Liaison, shortly after I moved into the SJU Residence for my first year at Waterloo. When I joined Students' Union, it was the first time I saw what a student group was capable of accomplishing. It presented a stark contrast with my previous experinces in high school, where only initial ideas, some planning, and mostly gruntwork were the responsibilies of students. I saw and was part of a group responsible for thousands of dollars of student money, that manages an online store, organizes an event for hundreds, and constanty seeks to improve the lives of students on campus.

As I enter my tenure as Chief Financial Officer, I striving to modernize and optimize the way in which we organize ourselves and how we conduct buisness in order to better serve our students.
